Support

Need help with TagNote? This page covers the most common questions and how to reach us. TagNote is a tag-first note-taking app available on the web and on iPhone and iPad.

Contact us

The fastest way to get help is by email. We aim to respond within a few business days.

support@bluelight.ventures

Please include the device and app version (Settings shows the version) and a short description of the problem.

Getting started

  • Create an account with your email and a password, or sign in with Google on the web.
  • Write a note, then add tags to it. Tags are how you organize and find everything in TagNote.
  • Use search and tag filters to find any note instantly.
  • On iPhone and iPad, open the app and sign in to the same account to access your notes everywhere.

Managing your account

Reset your password. On the login screen choose "Forgot password?" and follow the email instructions. You can also use "Login without password" to receive a magic sign-in link.

Export your data. You can export all of your notes, tags, and settings at any time from the app.

Delete your account. To permanently delete your account and all associated notes, tags, and settings:

  1. Open the TagNote app and go to Settings.
  2. Tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm. This is immediate and cannot be undone.

If you cannot access the app, email us at the address above and we will delete your account for you.

Common questions

Is my data private? Yes. Your notes and tags are private to your account and are never sold or shared. See our Privacy Policy for details.

I found a bug or have a feature request. Email us at support@bluelight.ventures with the details and we will take a look.
